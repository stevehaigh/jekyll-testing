My take on the best ski runs in the Alps. This idea is inspired by the book 50 Classic Ski Descents of North America.

The selection is entirely subjective and is very much a work in progress. Classic routes include pistes runs, lift-accessed off-piste, lines that need to be hiked, multi day ski tours and anything else I can think of. This is not a guide, I don't give grades or any kind of accurate assessement, and I certainly don't claim to have skied them all. It's just a bit of fun and I'd love to add more routes to the list, so please feel free to contribute.
